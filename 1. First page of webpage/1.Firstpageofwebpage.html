BERA 

GROUP

 The Bera group research revolves around Organometallic Chemistry – ligand design incorporating desired functionalities, synthesis of new metal complexes, studying fundamental chemistry and applications of these compounds in different areas. A large part of our activity is dedicated to the catalytic evaluation for reactions that are synthetically challenging and directly address chemical aspects of renewable energy and sustainability. Understanding the mechanism of a catalytic reaction is a key undertaking in our program. Further, we seek to extend the use of these compounds in biology and material chemistry.

 

In addition to a modern synthetic organometallic laboratory, we have access to a host of experimental techniques. Computational tools are often exploited to support the mechanistic proposal. We strive to gain a deeper understanding of important chemical reactions through a unifying approach utilizing different tools and techniques.
